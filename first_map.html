My Favorite Restaurants in West Palm Beach

By Zarin Ismail

This map shows four of my favorite restaurants in my hometown, West Palm Beach, and their proximity between each other. I love eating all kinds of food, and I find myself frequenting these places whenever I crave certain cuisines. Three of these are dine-in restaurants, while the other one, Jaws Hot Chicken and Seafood, is a grab-and-go shop.
