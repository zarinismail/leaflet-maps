Public High Schools in Palm Beach County

By Zarin Ismail

This map shows the 32 public high schools part of the Palm Beach County School District. The district boundaries stretch from Jupiter down to Boca Raton and include other cities like West Palm Beach, Loxahatchee, Pahokee, Lake Worth Beach and Delray Beach. Some of the schools shown are also alternative education, adult education, K-12 or middle-high schools. Above Jupiter begins the Martin County School District and below Boca Raton starts Broward County Public Schools.

A list of the schools can be found on the Palm Beach County School District website.
